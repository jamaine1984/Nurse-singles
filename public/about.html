About

Healthcare dating built around real schedules and privacy.

Nurse Singles is a dating and community app for adults in healthcare: nurses, doctors, dental professionals, nursing students, travel clinicians, allied health workers, technicians, assistants, and hospital staff.

The app exists because healthcare workers often date around schedules that standard dating apps do not understand. Night shifts, rotating weekends, clinical rotations, travel assignments, emergency call, and post-shift recovery all affect when someone can talk, meet, or start a video intro.

What makes Nurse Singles different

Shift-aware matching

Profiles can include day, night, rotating, flexible, and preferred dating windows so users can find people who respect healthcare timing.

Workplace privacy

Members can hide employer details, avoid same-workplace matching, block users, and keep credential details private.

Healthcare community

The Nurse Hub, feed, and speed dating rooms are designed around healthcare roles, safe introductions, and respectful conversation.

Safety and responsible use

Nurse Singles is not medical advice, not a place to share protected patient information, and not affiliated with any hospital system unless a partner is publicly identified. Users should not post patient names, room numbers, images, employer-confidential information, license numbers, or private identity documents.

Public safety standards, privacy policy, terms, account deletion instructions, and child safety standards are available without logging in. The app is for adults and includes reporting and blocking tools for safety concerns.

Public resources

Before creating an account, visitors can read practical healthcare dating guides in the Nurse Hub, review Privacy, read the Terms of Service, and contact support through the Contact page.
