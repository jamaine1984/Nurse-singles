Account and Data Deletion

Effective date: April 30, 2026

This page explains how Nurse Singles International users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

Delete Your Account in the App

Open Nurse Singles International, sign in, go to Settings, and use the account deletion option. This removes the account profile from the app and starts deletion of associated personal data.

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, email mattressvibrations@gmail.com from the email address used for your account. Include the subject line "Nurse Singles account deletion request" and the account email address.

What Is Deleted

Deletion requests cover your account profile, profile photos, gallery photos, posts, messages where deletion is technically and legally permitted, preferences, and app activity associated with your account.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited records when required for legal obligations, payment records, chargeback or fraud prevention, abuse prevention, safety investigations, security logs, or compliance with app-store and law-enforcement requirements.

Delete Some Data Without Deleting Your Account

You may request deletion of specific personal data without deleting your account by emailing mattressvibrations@gmail.com. Describe the data you want removed and include your account email address.
