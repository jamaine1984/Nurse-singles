Who it serves

A dating app designed for healthcare roles

Generic dating apps often miss the details that matter to healthcare workers: compressed sleep windows, weekend rotations, workplace boundaries, agency assignments, clinical placements, and the need to avoid sharing patient or employer information publicly.

Nurses and nursing students

Profiles can show shift type, nursing student status, preferred dating windows, and privacy choices around school, clinicals, or workplace details.

Travel clinicians

Travel nurses and contract clinicians can highlight assignment dates, city hubs, time zones, and whether they want local or longer-distance connections.

Dental and allied health

Dental hygienists, dentists, assistants, therapists, techs, and support staff belong in the same healthcare community, with role badges controlled by the user.

Doctors and hospital staff

Profession-aware matching can respect departments, boundaries, verification status, and the need to keep workplace identity private.

Editorial standards

Useful healthcare guidance, not scaled location pages

Nurse Hub does not publish copied news, patient stories, medical advice, or dozens of near-identical city pages. Articles are written for a specific reader decision and link to primary sources when discussing professional guidance or scam prevention.

Product standards

Healthcare-friendly features and boundaries

The app is designed for adults and is not a place to share protected patient information. Its dating features are organized around personal compatibility, safety, and privacy rather than clinical advice or medical services.

  • Profession badges that users can choose to show or hide.
  • Shift-aware matching for day, night, rotating, and flexible schedules.
  • Workplace privacy controls for hospital, clinic, department, and coworker visibility.
  • Mutual consent follow-ups after speed dating rooms.
  • Block and report flows for safety concerns.
  • Separate public standards for child safety and account deletion.
  • Article content for healthcare dating, nurse lifestyle, and international community topics.
  • Web app access for desktop, tablet, Android, and iPhone home-screen installs.
