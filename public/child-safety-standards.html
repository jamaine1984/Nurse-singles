Child Safety Standards

Effective date: May 4, 2026

Nurse Singles International is an adult dating and community app for healthcare workers, healthcare staff, and nursing students. The app is not intended for children, and users must be at least 18 years old to create an account or use the service.

Zero Tolerance for CSAE and CSAM

We prohibit child sexual abuse and exploitation, child sexual abuse material, grooming, sexualization of minors, trafficking, coercion, extortion, solicitation involving minors, and any attempt to share, request, promote, or normalize this content or behavior.

Age and Access Standards

  • Users must be 18 or older.
  • Profiles, posts, messages, photos, reports, and account activity may be reviewed for safety and policy compliance.
  • Accounts suspected of involving minors, impersonating minors, or attempting to contact minors for sexual purposes may be restricted, removed, and reported.

Content and Conduct Standards

  • Users may not upload, post, send, request, or link to CSAM or sexual content involving minors.
  • Users may not use coded language, links, off-platform contact requests, or third-party services to evade these standards.
  • Users may not threaten, blackmail, exploit, groom, or pressure any person into sexual activity or sharing intimate content.
  • Users may not post naked or sexually explicit images. Profile, chat, and community images are subject to safety review.

Detection, Review, and Enforcement

We use a combination of user reports, account review, automated safety checks, image moderation, and administrative tools to help identify and remove prohibited content. Enforcement actions may include content removal, account suspension, account termination, preservation of relevant records, and reports to appropriate regional or national authorities when required or appropriate.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report child safety concerns in the app from user profiles, messages, posts, and other safety/reporting surfaces. Reports are reviewed for potential policy violations and safety escalation.

For urgent safety or law enforcement matters, contact the appropriate emergency services or law enforcement agency in your jurisdiction.

Legal Compliance and External Reporting

Nurse Singles International complies with applicable child safety laws and cooperates with lawful requests from regional and national authorities. When we become aware of apparent CSAM or child sexual exploitation activity, we may preserve relevant information and report to the appropriate authorities as required by law.
